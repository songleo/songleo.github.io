- 通过api检验hub的健康状态

```
curl https://hub.test.com/api/galaxy/pulp/api/v3/status/
```

- 通过api获取hub的role

```
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer xxxxxx" https://hub.test.com/api/galaxy/pulp/api/v3/roles/
```

- hub api文档

